Profile Overview

I, Amai T.D Ngulube, am a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) student at the University of Pretoria with a growing foundation in law, media, marketing, and public discourse. My academic journey and professional experiences have allowed me to develop strong analytical, communication, and research skills, alongside a deep appreciation for the role law plays in shaping society and creating meaningful impact.

Beyond my legal studies, I have built practical experience through editorial, administrative, and leadership roles in both professional and student-led environments. My work with PDBY Media, the National Prosecuting Authority, and various university organisations, including BLASC, UNICEF and UNASA UP, have strengthened my ability to communicate effectively, think critically, manage responsibilities under pressure, and collaborate with diverse teams.

I am passionate about combining legal knowledge with clear communication and public engagement to contribute thoughtfully and professionally in every environment I am part of. My background in media and public communication has also given me valuable experience in research, editorial writing, digital communication, and audience engagement. These experiences continue to shape my approach to leadership, problem-solving, and ethical decision-making.

Alongside my studies, I have completed certifications in areas including financial accounting, international law, political science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and project management. These multidisciplinary interests reflect my commitment to continuous learning and adapting to an evolving professional landscape.

I am fluent in multiple South African languages, including English, Afrikaans, Sepedi, Southern Sotho, isiZulu, and TshiVenda, which has strengthened my ability to connect with people from diverse backgrounds and engage in meaningful dialogue across different communities.
